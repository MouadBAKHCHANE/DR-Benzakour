Faq’s

Frequently Asked Questions

Q.1
How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by visiting our website and filling out the online form with your preferred date, time, and contact details.

Q.2
What should I expect during my first visit to Medenic?

During your first visit, you'll be asked to provide your medical history. A doctor will conduct a thorough consultation, which may include lab tests if needed.

Q.3
Do you offer telemedicine consultations?

Yes, we offer convenient telemedicine consultations for a range of medical concerns. Patients can connect with our doctors from the comfort of their home through phone appointments.

Q.4
Are vaccinations available for adults and children?

Yes, we provide a full range of vaccinations for both adults and children, including routine immunizations and travel vaccines.

Q.5
What documents should I bring for my visit?

Please bring a valid ID, medical records (if available), current medications list, and your insurance or health card for a smooth check-in process.

Q.6
Do you accept walk-ins, or is an appointment required?

Yes, we gladly accept walk-ins during clinic hours, but booking an appointment in advance is recommended to reduce waiting times and ensure prompt attention.
